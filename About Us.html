About Us

Founded in 1878, Hayleys has grown into one of the most respected and diversified business entities in Sri Lanka. The company plays a key role in the country's economy, delivering innovative solutions to local and global markets.

Our businesses span across a range of industries including agriculture, transportation, logistics, leisure, and more. We are committed to sustainability and social responsibility, driving value to our customers, stakeholders, and communities.

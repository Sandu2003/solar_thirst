In this section, you can view and edit the customer process flow steps. Each step can be modified with a description and day.

Process Flow Steps

Step 1: Request Service

Once you fill out the service request form, our team will evaluate your needs and recommend the most suitable installation plan based on your requirements.

Step 2: Quotation

After your service request is reviewed, you will receive a customized quotation. The quotation will include details of the installation plan, costs, and any additional services that might be required.

Step 3: Confirmation

Once you approve the quotation, our team will confirm the schedule for installation. You will also be informed of the necessary documents required (e.g., payment confirmation, ID card copy, etc.).

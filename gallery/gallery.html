Located in the sunny district of Hambantota, this solar project aims to supply clean energy to over 500 households. The Kandy Green Power Project, situated near the picturesque Kandy Lake, harnesses solar energy to provide eco-friendly electricity to nearby schools and hospitals. Galle Eco Solar Project

The Galle Eco Solar Project is strategically located in the coastal city of Galle, providing clean energy to local fishing communities. The initiative showcases the potential of renewable energy in enhancing local economies.

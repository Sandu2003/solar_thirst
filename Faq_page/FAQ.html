How do solar panels work?

They take sunlight and use it to create electricity through a process called the photovoltaic effect. When sunlight hits the panels, it creates an electric current.

How do I maintain solar panels?

Solar panels require very little maintenance. Just keep them clean and clear of debris, and they will work well for a long time.
